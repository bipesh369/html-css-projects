Gautam Buddha

The Light of Peace and Enlightenment

Life Timeline

563 BCE: Born in Lumbini, present-day Nepal.

Early Life: Lived as Prince Siddhartha Gautama.

Age 29: Renounced royal life in search of truth.

Enlightenment: Attained enlightenment in Bodh Gaya.

Parinirvana: Passed away at the age of 80.

Teachings and Legacy

  • Founder of Buddhism
  • Taught peace, compassion, and non-violence
  • Introduced the Eightfold Path
  • Inspired millions across the world
  • Lumbini recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site
